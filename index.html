About Me

Hello, I'm Elena

I'm a freelance model specializing in modest fashion, working with brands that value elegance, sophistication, and timeless style. My journey in fashion began as a personal exploration of self-expression while staying true to my values.

With over five years of experience in the industry, I've had the privilege of collaborating with designers, photographers, and brands who share my vision of modest fashion as a powerful form of creative expression.

When I'm not in front of the camera, I'm passionate about mentoring young models and writing about the intersection of faith, fashion, and femininity in today's world.
